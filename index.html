Jamshedpur

Jamshedpur is a city located in the Indian state of Jharkhand. It is known as the "Steel City" due to the presence of Tata Steel, one of the largest steel manufacturers in the country. Jamshedpur also has a number of parks, museums, and temples worth visiting.

Parks

Jamshedpur has many beautiful parks worth exploring.
Dams

Jamshedpur's dams, such as Dimna Lake, offer scenic views and boating options.
Temples

Jamshedpur's Sai Baba Temple is a popular religious destination for visitors.
Zoo

Jamshedpur's Tata Steel Zoological Park features a variety of wildlife species.
